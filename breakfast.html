Breakfast

My favorite quick breakfasts that fuel your body and energize you for the day.

Egg Plate

Heres what you need for this simple breakfast (p.s. this is great for fridge clean out!)

  • 2 Eggs
  • Any veggies you have (I used arugula and onions)
  • Bread (I used sandwich bread)
  • Jam, PB, or other spread
  • An extra plate filler! Choose some meat, fruit, pastry, or whatever you have on hand to complete the plate.

Steps:

  1. Chop up and sautee your veggies.
  2. Scramble your eggs in a bowl and then add to the pan with the veggies.
  3. Cook until the eggs are done to your liking.
  4. Toast your bread and add your favorite spread.
  5. Grab your plate filler!
  6. Plate it up and enjoy.

Yogurt Bowl

This breakfast comes together in literally 5 minutes and is super filling, it's been my go to all year.

  • Greek Yogurt (I like plain whole milk)
  • Honey
  • Fresh or frozen fruit (I like frozen blueberries)
  • Granola, nuts, seeds, or other toppings. (I generally use almonds, chia seeds, shredded coconut, and granola)

Steps:

  1. Put about 3/4 cups of yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Stir in a drizzle of honey.
  3. Add your granola, seeds, etc.
  4. Stir it up.
  5. Add your berries.
  6. Stir again and enjoy.
