Fancy Grilled Cheese

There are so many variations of this sandwich, and they make me want to cry they are so good every time.

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Cheese (I like sharp cheddar, but use any type)
  • Lunch meat if you like it
  • Spinach or arugula
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced avocado
  • Honey mustard

Steps:

  1. Sautee your veggies.
  2. Spread butter or oil on the outside of both slices of bread.
  3. Place on piece of bread in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Put the cheese on and then top with the hot veggies.
  5. Heat the meat in the pan for a few seconds and then add it on top.
  6. Spread the honey mustard on the other piece of bread.
  7. Place the top piece of bread on and cook until both sides are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Enjoy!

Salad With Eggs

This is one of my favorite things to make when I don't want meat but I do want protein.

  • 2 Eggs
  • Lettuce
  • ANY salad toppings- go crazy
  • Salad dressing

Steps:

  1. Fill a bowl 3/4 full of lettuce (literally any kind).
  2. Fry 2 eggs and put them on top.
  3. Put on whatever toppings you like (cheese, nuts, seeds, onions, other veggies, the option are endless)
  4. Add your favorite dressing
  5. Serve with crackers or bread and eat up!
