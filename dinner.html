Dinner

I like to meal prep my dinners, so these are flavor packed meals that are great for that. You'll look forward to eating them at the end of the day.

Chicken, Pasta, and Veggies

This dinner has so much flavor, it's cheap, and it's so good and filling. An all time fav of mine.

  • Pasta (any shape)
  • Any veggies you have (I used spinach, broccoli and onions)
  • Chicken breast
  • 2 Lemons

Steps:

  1. Cover your chicken with lemon jucie, salt, olive oil, and garlic powder to taste. Cook it in a skillet over medium until there is no pink in the middle. Then chop it up into bite sized pieces.
  2. Chop up your veggies and sautee them until soft in the same skillet you cooked your chicken in.
  3. Cook your pasta by the instructions on the bag.
  4. Put your cooked pasta, veggies, and chicken in the pan with some lemon juice and salt and cook until hot and combined.
  5. Plate it up and enjoy

Meal Prep Tacos

These tacos are SO good I could eat them every day, and they are super easy to put together.

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado
  • Lime
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Cook your ground beef in a skillet until done, then remove the grease and add a little bit of water and all the taco seasoning.
  2. Chop the tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and avocado and add them to a large bowl with the corn, lime juice, and some salt and stir to combine.
  3. Heat your tortillas (you can do this super fast in the toaster)
  4. Load up the taco with everything, add cheese and hot sauce and enjoy!
