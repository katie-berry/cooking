Single Serve Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

These cookies are INSANE. Thank you Broma Bakery for providing me with this recipe. I will make these atleast once a wekk until the day I die.

  • 4 T brown sugar
  • 1 T white sugar
  • 3 T butter
  • 1 t vanilla extract
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/8 t baking soda
  • 1/4 t salt
  • Chocolate chips to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  3. Brown the butter on the stove over medium heat, stirring constantly until the bubbles die down and it smells sweet and rich.
  4. Add the butter to a seperate bowl with the sugars, vanilla, and egg yolk.
  5. Stir in the dry ingredients.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Bake for 12 minutes and enjoy!

Frozen Yogurt Bites

These are an internet sensation and trust me, they are worth the hype.

  • Greek Yogurt (I like plain whole milk)
  • Honey
  • Chia seeds
  • Chopped fruit (I used banana)
  • Chocolate chips
  • Coconut or canola oil

Steps:

  1. Put about 1/2 cup of yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Stir in 1 T of honey and the chia seeds.
  3. Stir in the fruit.
  4. Scoop onto a baking sheet lined with parchment in clumps.
  5. Freeze for 1 hour.
  6. Heat your chocolate and oil until melted, stirring every 15 seconds.
  7. Dunk each frozen bite in chocolate and place back on the baking sheet.
  8. Freeze for another hour and enjoy!
